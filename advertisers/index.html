01 / Operational context

A routable offer is an operational specification

The landing URL alone is not enough. The network needs supported GEOs, devices, traffic restrictions, conversion definitions, payout values, caps, validation rules and technical integration details.

02 / Operational context

Distribution should begin with controlled volume

Early traffic establishes baseline conversion and quality. Caps can then expand when reporting, approval and downstream signals reconcile.

03 / Operational context

Feedback improves traffic allocation

Advertiser status updates and payout values help the routing layer distinguish raw registrations from payable business outcomes.
