01 / Operational context

What a dating CPA network actually coordinates

A dating CPA network sits between traffic and advertiser demand. The operational job is not merely to provide a tracking URL. It must maintain offer eligibility, conversion definitions, GEO and device coverage, attribution, payout data, validation rules and partner communication.

02 / Operational context

Why a SmartLink-first model matters

A direct offer can work when the buyer already knows the audience and has enough volume to optimize one funnel. A SmartLink is useful when traffic is broader, mixed across GEOs or fragmented across placements. The routing layer evaluates available signals before selecting an eligible destination.

03 / Operational context

Publishers, agencies and advertisers see different parts of the same system

Publishers need stable tracking and monetization. Agencies need source-level reporting and scalable links. Advertisers need qualified traffic, clean attribution and transparent event definitions. The network must make these requirements compatible.

SmartLink routing does not replace transparent traffic acquisition, stable tracking or partner communication. It makes those inputs more useful by connecting them to eligible dating inventory.
