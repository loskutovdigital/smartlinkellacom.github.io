01 / Operational context

Inventory context matters

A banner on a dating-related property differs from broad run-of-network inventory. Site and placement IDs should remain visible.

02 / Operational context

Creative and destination should align

The SmartLink can select among eligible routes, but the banner should not promise a category that the route pool cannot support.

03 / Operational context

Viewability is separate from conversion quality

Low engagement may reflect placement exposure rather than offer quality. Media and conversion metrics should be reviewed together.
