FAQ

Questions publishers actually ask.

What GEOs does Smartlinkella cover?
All Tier 1, 2, and 3 GEOs. If your traffic carries a country code, the SmartLink has a route for it.
What traffic types are accepted?
Push, pop/redirect, native, social, search/SEO, and in-app. Running something outside this list? Talk to us before you apply.
How fast is publisher approval?
Most applications are reviewed within one business day.
Do you work with solo publishers or only agencies?
Both. Solo publishers get a self-serve SmartLink; agencies get custom flows and a dedicated onboarding process.
Is there a minimum volume requirement?
No fixed minimum. We onboard at any volume and scale support as you grow.
How does payment work?
Net-7 payout cycle by default, via wire, USDT, Wise, or Payoneer. Volume-based terms are available on request.
