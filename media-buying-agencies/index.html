01 / Operational context

Agency traffic requires operational separation

A single agency account may contain several buyers, platforms, GEOs and creative strategies. The link structure must preserve those differences instead of blending all clicks into one report.

02 / Operational context

Routing does not replace source optimization

Smartlinkella optimizes eligible destinations. The agency remains responsible for acquisition cost, creative quality, compliance and source-level decisions.

03 / Operational context

Reporting should support buyer accountability

Buyer, source, campaign and placement identifiers should be mapped before volume is launched. This makes discrepancies and quality changes easier to isolate.
