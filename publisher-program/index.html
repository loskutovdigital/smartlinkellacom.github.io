01 / Operational context

Who the publisher program is designed for

The program is intended for affiliates, site owners, audience operators and media buyers who can explain how traffic is generated and pass stable campaign identifiers.

02 / Operational context

What improves approval speed

A complete application includes primary traffic type, monthly volume, GEO distribution, device mix and a realistic description of the promotional method.

03 / Operational context

What happens after approval

Partners receive the routing link and tracking requirements, validate the first clicks and postbacks, then begin with controlled volume before scaling.
