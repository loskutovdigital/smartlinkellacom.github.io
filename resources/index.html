01 / Operational context

A network built around one routing product

Smartlinkella focuses on the dating vertical and one operational problem: deciding where each eligible click should go. This resource library explains the systems around that decision, including offer models, traffic-source characteristics, tracking, postbacks, quality controls and partner onboarding.

02 / Operational context

Choose the path that matches your role

Publishers can start with traffic monetization and accepted source guides. Agencies can review routing, subID structure and reporting. Advertisers can review validation, integration and traffic-quality expectations before discussing inventory.

Operational principle

SmartLink routing does not replace transparent traffic acquisition, stable tracking or partner communication. It makes those inputs more useful by connecting them to eligible dating inventory.
