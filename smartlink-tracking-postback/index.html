01 / Operational context

Click identity begins at the source

The traffic source or tracker generates a click ID. The publisher passes that identifier into the SmartLink, which associates it with the network click.

02 / Operational context

The postback must return business meaning

A conversion callback should distinguish event type, status and payout where supported. A raw registration and an approved payable lead are not the same optimization signal.

03 / Operational context

Reconciliation catches invisible failures

Network, tracker and source reports should be compared regularly for missing IDs, duplicates, delays and timezone differences.

Operational principle

SmartLink routing does not replace transparent traffic acquisition, stable tracking or partner communication. It makes those inputs more useful by connecting them to eligible dating inventory.
